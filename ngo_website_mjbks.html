About Us

MJBKS is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering women and uplifting children through education, skill development, and community-driven programs. We strive to build a society where every woman is empowered and every child has access to quality education and a dignified life.

Our Programs

  • Skill Development: Tailoring, handicrafts, and folk art training for women.
  • Child Education: Bridge schools and tutoring for underprivileged children.
  • Health & Hygiene: Menstrual hygiene awareness, nutrition, and maternal health camps.
  • Art & Culture: Promoting regional art forms like Madhubani to generate livelihoods.
